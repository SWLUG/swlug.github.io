South Wales Linux User Group

Welcome to the SWLUG homepage! SWLUG are an informal, friendly group of Linux and Free Software enthusiasts who meet monthly at various venues throughout South Wales - currently:

We exist to support local users of the Linux operating system and other Free Software. To this end, we run a mailing list, IRC channel and, very occasionally, organise talks.

We're always happy to welcome anyone to join our meetings, have discussions on the mailing list or a chat in the IRC channel - whatever your interest or experience level.

To find out more, such as where we're meeting next, see the mailing list, get in touch on IRC or visit G+.
